Bio

Adil Hussain is currently a Senior Research Fellow pursuing an Integrated Ph.D. in Arts and Humanities at the University of Kashmir. Specialising in postdigital humanities and cultural studies, his research explores the intersection of identity, narrativity and social media in contemporary fiction. With a strong teaching background, he has taught English literature, communication skills and functional English. His work adopts a multidisciplinary approach reflected in publications in SCOPUS-indexed and Web of Science indexed journals and presentations at national and international conferences.
